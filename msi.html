IIT Bombay Mars Rover Team


The IITB Mars Rover team comprises IIT Bombay students that work towards building a prototype Mars rover capable of extra-terrestrial robotics. I have been working in the Electronics and Software subsystem on the team sinnce December 2017. The team competed in URC 2018 and stood at 31st position.

My work in the team has been in the areas of obstacle detection and stereocamera vision. With my teammates, we developed a Gaussian Mixture Model for color segmentation to detect the presence of tennis ball detection which will be later for autonomous path planning as the tennis balls are used as markers in the rover path. I wrote a script to wirelessly control the gopro camera with raspberry pi. I also worked in the implementation of stereovision for the rover for distance legend mapping required in the bioscience task of the competition. Initially, I tried it via image processing techniques but now we are looking to implement the concept with Capsule networks.
