Show Genesis Pvt. Ltd

Show Genesis Pvt. Ltd. is a startup that develops hardware and software solutions for different purposes. I did an internship here during the month of December where my co-intern and I worked to develop a single server to control multiple crazyflie nanodrones for indoor navigation on ROS framework. We used the location positioning node repository of crazyflie and modified to our purpose. The location was determined with multiple anchors across the room and a tag node was attached to the quadcopter. The location accuracy was further imporved with multiple filters implemented in the crazyflie repository. We tested for the autonomous takeoff, hover and landing of 2 drones from a server.
