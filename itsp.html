Institute Technical Summer Project

We developed a human detection hexacopter, with on-board camera module that can be used to take consecutive photos and detected the presence of humans in the images.

The hardware of the drone (F550 frame, motors, ESCs, wires, zippers, propellers) was collected from MAV lab in Aerospace department, IIT Bombay. The drone was assembled and had a + configuration. For control system of the drone, we used ardupilot mega 2.6, which is a professional quality IMU autopilot that is based on the Arduino Mega platform. The control board was attached with a GPS module for navigation of the drone and caliberated with the configurations of the hexacopter with a mission planner software, qGroundControl.

For human detection part, we had a Raspberry pi with a camera module which continuously and periodically captured images which were transferred to the laptop at ground station. After receiving the images, we used a human detection classifier with HOG descriptor and Support Vector Machines in openCV.


The Final report and codes can be found in the github repository: ITSP.
