Warehouse Inventory Check

Warehouse Inventory Check is a competition organiesed by Honeywell in the Inter-IIT Tech Contingent meet 2018, held in IIT Madras. The objective was to develop a UAV which ca fly indoors and had to autonomously fly from starting point to end point, scanning the inventory boxes, updating their location and extracting QR code, barcode and possibly warning sign on the ground station. The problem statement can be found here.

For indoor navigation, we used pixhawk px4 with optical flow camera which used the ground texture for guidance. The data from optical flow camera and control board were obtained by ROS framework using mavros. For QR code and barcode extraction, we had a camera module which was controlled via odroid XU4. The region of interest was extracted using contour detection. For example, barcodes have high horizaontal gradients and low vertical gradients so we took sobel gradients of the image and took the difference of to get contours corresponding to the region of barcode. The warning sign was detected using shape detection as the sign was in a triangular shape.


The Final presentation and codes can be found in the github repository: Warehouse_Inventory (the repository will soon be completely updated).
