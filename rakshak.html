Software subsystem- Team Rakshak

Team Rakshak is an IIT Bombay student initiative to develop a fleet of robust Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) to support Search and Rescue Operations (SRO) in the event of disaster. The team's current aim is to participate in the AUVSI SUAS Competition.

From May 2017 to December 2017, I worked in the software subsystem of the team. This was amongst my first experience with image processing and Machine learning and my sort of my first technical work experience. I spent first few weeks to get comfortable with opencv, concepts of machine learning and working of the team. I developed a color segmentation algorithm using k-means clustering for detection of the region containing some shape in the image. In theory, we had to segment the image in 3 clusters- one for grass, one for the shape and one for letter inside the shape but due to disturbances in the grass, I used around 5 clusters and the algorithm did a good image segmentation job. I also worked in letter recognition using deep convolutional neural networks.
